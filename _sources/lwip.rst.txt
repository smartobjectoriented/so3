lwIP - Lightweigth IP
=====================

lwIP is a small independent implementation of the TCP/IP protocol suite.

Information about lwIP can be obtained on the `official site <https://savannah.nongnu.org/projects/lwip>`__.

Integration of lwIP in SO3
--------------------------
